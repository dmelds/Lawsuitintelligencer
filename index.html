About the Editor

Lawsuit Intelligencer is published and edited by David Meldofsky, a California-licensed attorney and the founder of Lawsuit Informer (plain-English public legal education) and Lawsuit Center (organized case-review information by lawsuit category).

Lawsuit Intelligencer is the editorial layer of that ecosystem — written for attorneys, journalists, and the firms working inside the litigation industry rather than the consumers reading about it.
